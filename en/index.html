The Eventum Vision

Unified Platform

A unified platform for effortless event and company discovery, designed to engage potential but currently solo users. Our core mission is to help people of all ages stop seeing event discovery as a barrier and make it easy and accessible.

All In One App

For users: Ad Free, filters by personal preferences and mood, built-in chatting and profiles.

For organizers: Reaching the most targeted audience, Deep Analytics, and the most competitive pricing.

Concept

  • ALL-IN-ONE: Ecosystem, a hub from house parties to concerts.
  • SOCIAL LAYER: Not just “Where to go”, but “Who to go with”.
  • BEYOND TICKETS: We provide not just tickets but entire communities.

Project Risks

⚠ Strategic Risks

  • • "Chicken and Egg" — at launch, neither audience nor organizers.
  • • Negative unit economics — customer acquisition might cost more than the ticket commission.
  • • Transaction Leakage — user finds an event with us, but pays the organizer directly.
  • • Strong Competitors — large players can quickly copy features and crush with budget.

🛡 Product and Reputational

  • • Security — offline meetings may carry risk of fraud or conflicts.
  • • Low Retention — user found company and moves to messengers.
  • • Content Quality — spam and weak events lower trust in the platform.

⚙ Operational and Technical

  • • Technical Failures — server overload or algorithm errors.
  • • Financial and Legal Complexities — issues with split payments and refunds.
  • • Team Dependency — founder burnout can slow down growth.
